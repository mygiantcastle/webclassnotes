There are two ways to link

    >
  1. The first way is to link to another page on our website--internal link
  2. The second way is to link to another page on the internet--external link

To create a hyperlink, we use an anchor tag which is just the letter "a".

It includes the anchor tag+the text to appear.

The opening anchor tag contains the anchor tag (="a") + "href="destination of link"
